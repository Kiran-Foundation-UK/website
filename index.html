Our Mission

Our mission is to support individuals from underprivileged and marginalised section of society in education and skill training, so they can reach their full potential. They are forced by circumstances to battle harsh realities of life at the cost of their future. We support such kids via following two programs:

Scholarship

We provide scholarship to students from underprivileged and marginalised families. We also assist them in finding and applying for various scholarships and higher education grants available via various organisations. Selection Process:
