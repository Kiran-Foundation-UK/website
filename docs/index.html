Never Stop Learning

Learning helps you become independent, self sufficient and contribute to the progress of society to best of your abilities.

What we do?

  • Scholarship

    Scholarships and grants for higher education amonng underprivilaged and bright youth believing in non-violence and living in UK and commonwealth countries.

  • Classes

    High quality free and affordable classes, workshops and webinars for Children and Young people, with practical and hands on training.

  • Trainings

    Advance Learning for people who are no longer in full time education or employment. We run short term Courses such as Web Designing, Digital Skills etc.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enable equal opportunities to young people from weaker section of society in the UK and commonwealth countries. We support individuals from underprivileged and marginalised section of society in education and skill training, so they can reach their full potential and contribute to the progress of society to best of their abilities..

Scholarship

We provide scholarship to students from underprivileged and marginalised families. We also assist them in finding and applying for various scholarships and higher education grants available via various organisations.

Workshops & Courses

We conduct skill training workshops that enhance employability. These are mainly digital skills that enhance not only employability but helps self employed in enhancing their business prospects.

Our Trainers

Our trainers are volunteers, who are passionate about more inclusive society and want to contribute towards an inclusive society using their skills.

We invite application for trainers and and our trainers go through an interview process. Selected volunteers are then go through training to conduct our courses.
